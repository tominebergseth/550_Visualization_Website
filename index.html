About Us

USC Data Science At Scale, Team 9, Spring 2024

We are a team of 6 USC Data Science Graduate Students who have worked on the Bigfoot Field Researchers Organization (BFRO) dataset thoughout the Spring 2024 semester to add relevant features, cluster, and analyze bigfoot sightings throughout the US.

This website contains five visualizations we made using D3.js to showcase some central findings of our dataset.
